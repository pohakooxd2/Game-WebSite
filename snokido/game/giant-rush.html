    Giant Rush!

    Author : TapNation  -   59 796 plays

    Race your giant in the casual arcade game Giant Rush Avoid obstacles, deadly traps and absorb stickmen of the right color to grow and gain power. At each end of the level you will have to face an opponent in a melee fight, try to win and push him back as far as possible with your final kick to get the best bonus. Regularly you will have to face formidable and strong bosses in intense fights which will require you to dodge attacks with the right timing and also to have enough power to triumph. You will also be able to collect purple crystals which will be used to improve your character or to buy cosmetic items in the store. Giant Rush features hundreds of levels for hours of gameplay!
