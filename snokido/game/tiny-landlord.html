    Tiny Landlord

    Author : PlayCade Interactive  -   17 957 plays

    In the simulation game Tiny Landlord you will become the mayor of a small town! It will be necessary to build, manage and develop residences, service buildings as well as facilities to bring in your first inhabitants. Watch out the crime rate thanks to the police, build parks, outdoor facilities and decorations for the happiness of your citizens. You will be able to unlock new districts that will allow you to expand your city even more by constructing other buildings there. It will be necessary to know how to control the city's budget in order to be able to develop it continuously.
