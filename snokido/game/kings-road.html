    Kings Road

    Author : Rumble   -   189 151 plays

    Kings Road is one of the greatest and finest browser-based games that exists! This Diablo-esque game is a skilful mix of RPG and battle game elements, set in a superbly made world. Play as an archer, knight or sorcerer and set off on an adventure either alone or with several friends to lend a hand in multi-player mode.
