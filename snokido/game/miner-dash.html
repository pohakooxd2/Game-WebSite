    Author : Teleponk   -   41 490 plays

    In the exploration game Miner Dash you will have to dig, dig deeper and deeper to reach the center of the earth! But time is running out because you will only have 60 days to complete your goal and the basic tools you have at the beginning will not be enough to pierce the deepest layers of the Earth's crust. Collect materials when you dig, the deeper you will come down the rarer and more valuable they will be.
