      2. Legal basis for the processing of personal data


      If you are a European user, your personal data is collected on the basis of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR, regulation number 2016/679). This means that we collect your data only when:

      • This is necessary for a legitimate interest (which is not supplanted by your individual privacy interest) such as for example to improve our services, prevent fraud or improve the security of our infrastructure.

      • When you have given your consent, for example for the diffusion of personalized advertisements, for the analysis of the nature and quality of our traffic on the site (pages visited, geographic or demographic data).

      We do not share your personal data related to your profile with third parties unless we are legally obliged to do so, or if you have given your explicit permission to do so. In the case of suspected fraud or abuse while using our website, we may transmit your personal data to the competent authorities.

      3. Data collection and processing:


      Data collection on the website Snokido.com is done in different ways:

      A. Data collected actively:

      • Registration:
        To create a user profile on Snokido.com, registration is necessary, we then store the e-mail address you entered on this occasion as well as the IP address linked to your account.

      • Profile:
        Your user profile may contain information that you have entered yourself, such as your age or your gender (this information can be changed at any time). By creating a profile, you also accept for example, that the number of games played, your last connection or the number of games rated are registered.
        To avoid abuse and fraud, it's not allowed to communicate your password to a third party.

      • Username and password:
        Your username is associated to a password. You are yourself responsible for handling your password with the greatest possible care. The user is reminded that the pseudonym alone does not constitute personal data.

      • Contact form:
        If you fill out a contact form or send us an email, the personal data you provide us on this occasion will be kept for as long as necessary for a complete response relating to the nature of the problem or the request you have made.

      • Storage duration:
        When you create a profile on Snokido.com we actively store the data you have provided to us for as long as your profile is used or until you tell us that you want to delete your profile.


      B. Data collected passively:

      • Use of cookies:
        Snokido uses "cookies" which are saved on the user's computer. A cookie is a small text file stored on your computer's hard drive or temporarily stored in your computer's memory. There are two types of cookies: session and persistent. Session cookies are deleted from your computer when you close your browser. Persistent cookies store information for later use.

      • Necessary and functional data:
        These necessary and functional cookies are activated when you visit the website and use the basic functions. These functions can be, for example, automatic connection, the saving of your favorite games or the customization of your profile. These cookies are automatically stored without obligatory consent, they guarantee the proper functioning of the site.

      • Third party data:
        These cookies are stored after your consent, thanks to them we can display advertisements adapted to your interests or measure the traffic on the site. For this we use the services of google:

        Google Analytics allows us to count visitors on a page and to collect information relating to the user's navigation on the site (the pages consulted, the date and time of the consultation). You can read the Google Analytics Privacy Policy here

        We use Google Adsense for advertisements. When you have given your consent, Google Adsense can increase the relevance of the ads served to you with personalized ads. You can read the Google Adsense Privacy Policy here


      C. Disabling cookies:
      Cookies can be disabled manually by the user. Below you will find a non-exhaustive list of the methods to use according to the different most used browsers:

      • Google Chrome:
        Click on the options menu at the top right of your browser (symbolized by three dots) then select "more tools" followed by "clear browsing data" (shortcuts Ctrl + Shift + Del). More information on disabling most technologies can be provided through the help section of your browser.

      • Mozilla Firefox:
        Click on the options menu at the top right of your browser, then select options (the icon with the cogwheel). Then click on the privacy and security tab, then go to the Cookies and site data section. More information on disabling most technologies can be provided through the help section of your browser.

      • Internet Explorer:
        Select the "tools" menu (Alt + X shortcuts) of your browser then click on the "Internet options" button. More information on disabling most technologies can be provided through the help section of your browser.
