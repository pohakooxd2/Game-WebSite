    String Theory 2

    Author : Lukas Donkers  -   45 791 plays

    In the game String Theory 2 you will have to solve puzzles with ingenuity to help the researcher Eve save her companion. Immerse yourself in the world of science and explore another dimension of space with extraordinary physical properties. Each level will offer you a challenge in which you will have to use different ropes to send an energy triangle to a black hole. Eve's experiences will become increasingly difficult to resolve, and will be accompanied by an evolving storytelling that teaches you more about the story as you progress.

    String Theory 2 was created by Lukas Donkers, a developer who also recently worked on the gameplay for the platformer Ori and the Will of the Wisps.
