    Dynamons 4

    Author : Agame  -   98 769 plays

    Get ready for an epic grand adventure in Dynamons 4, the long-awaited sequel to the famous turn-based RPG. Train your Dynamons and win battles, explore a huge world made up of varied regions and face formidable opponents along your journey. You can compose your team by capturing many Dynamons with different powers, the fights will be tactical because each creature will use an element and can be countered by another element. You will have to learn the strengths and weaknesses of all the Dynamons in order to defeat the strongest opponents! The adventure will offer many hours of gameplay and will be filled with mystery and secrecy. Your Dynamons will be able to gain levels, obtain new powers and also evolve. During your adventure you can try to find the rarest Dynamons to complete your collection!
