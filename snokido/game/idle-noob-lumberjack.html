    Idle Noob Lumberjack

    Author : Efim Maksimov  -   17 390 plays

    Idle Noob Lumberjack is a simulation and resource collection game in which you will have to build different buildings on your land and constantly explore the surrounding areas in search of resources. Mine wood and stone, sell your goods at the market to acquire gold and improve your buildings to increase their yield and productivity. You will be able to hire "minions" who automatically collect resources. Expand your land by buying more plots with gold and mine increasingly valuable resources. Idle Noob Lumberjack will then turn into a management game in which you will have to organize your production and your collection of resources as well as possible in order to meet all your needs and continue to grow.
