    Geometry Dash PC

    Author : RobTop  -   193 158 plays

    Discover one of the most popular mobile games with this version of Geometry Dash PC. This platform game offers you to lead a cube that slides on the floor and you will have to cross various obstacles and platforms while avoiding the deadly traps. Based on a Die and Retry mechanism, Geometry Dash PC will ask you a great deal of composure and total control of your small cube to advance as far as possible. Fail and restart again, this is the motto of the game and to face the great difficulty proposed by the app you will need to train really hard.
