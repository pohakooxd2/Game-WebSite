    Super Bandit RIP

    Author : Zetoman77  -   56 834 plays

    Super Bandit RIP is a multiplayer fighting game in which you will have to challenge online opponents in intense arena clashes! You will be able to choose from 12 playable characters, all with a different weapon and having their own special skills. 4 heroes will be available as standard, you can play 4 additional heroes by registering for free and the other 4 characters must be unlocked by gaining levels. Super Bandit RIP offers 4 game modes, Free For All, Melee Fighter, Ranged Warfare and Bib B Mania. Train to master a character and become the king of the arena! In addition to special attacks, heroes will be able to double jump, use a dash but also block projectiles. The matches will oppose 5 players online and will be fast and tactical. You can create personalized lobbies and invite your friends to join your game with a simple code.
