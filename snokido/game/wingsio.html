    Wings.io

    Author : Profusion Studios   -   358 128 plays

    Fly a fighter plane and try to shoot the other players in the multiplayer game Wings.io. At full speed in the air, war will rage and your survival will depend on your quickness and your flying skills. Dodge enemy fire, chase your targets and try to kill as many opponents to reach the top places in the overall ranking of your game. In flight you can find upgrades for your weapons but also health boost and speed bonus. The parties are usually short, intense and will leave you no respite.

    How to play Wings.io?Move with your mouse and shoot your opponents with the left button or the space key. Try to aim your best taking into account the speed and trajectory of your enemies. Be careful collisions with other players will destroy your plane!

    How to invit a friend on Wings.io?To invite friends to play in your game, click the 'Copy Room Link' button at the top left of the home screen. A link will then be generated, you just have to copy it and send it to your friends so that they can join you.

    Who is the author of Brutal.io?The game was created by Profusion Studios, the same development studio as Powerline.io and Brutal.io.
