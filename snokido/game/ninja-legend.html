    Ninja Legend

    Author : Yizhiyuan Network   -   47 451 plays

    In Ninja Legend you will have to face ruthless opponents using your javelin which will be a formidable weapon when properly handled. Aim accurately and throw your javelin in the direction of opponents to eliminate them, you can also parry their projectiles with your weapon. Explore a world full of dangers during a long journey that will take you to different regions. You will face experienced warriors, powerful mages but also fearsome bosses! Your hero will be able to improve his skills, gain levels and obtain valuable equipment that will strengthen his power. Each level can be completed in three different difficulties, easy, normal and hard.
