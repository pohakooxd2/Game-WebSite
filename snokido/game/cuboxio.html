    Cubox.io

    Author : Rafras  -   7 719 plays

    Cubox.io is a multiplayer online game inspired by Minecraft, Tibia, Albion and Diablo. Join a gigantic open world, fight monsters, explore dungeons and get ever more powerful equipment. You can form groups and cooperate with other players to face the most powerful bosses, but also join a guild and participate in expeditions. By activating the PVP mod the exploration of the world will combine with intense fights because other players will become your opponents! Cubox also offers a Battle Royale mod in which you will have to survive as long as possible against other players on a shrinking map. The construction mod will allow you to build your house and place trophies and other decorations there. Your house will be in a special neighborhood and can be seen and visited by other players.
