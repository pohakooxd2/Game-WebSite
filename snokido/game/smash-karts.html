    Smash Karts

    Author : Tall Team  -   490 878 plays

    Drive your kart on a closed karting track and try to neutralize the other players in Smash Karts, an online multiplayer game. Like in the battle mode of Mario Kart, you will need to collect the bonuses on the track, they will give you weapons to destroy other players karts. Each round lasts 3 minutes, try to score as many points as possible to take the first place of the leaderboard.
