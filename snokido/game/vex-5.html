    Vex 5

    Author : Amazing Adam   -   200 287 plays

    Take on ever more intense challenges in the platform game Vex 5 and its many new levels. Run, jump, push crates, climb ropes, slide along walls and more! Control your stickman with precision and accuracy to overcome the countless obstacles that will line the levels, hundreds of traps will await your mistakes to end your hero's race. In addition to being agile, it will be necessary to be as fast as possible to hope to win one of the three medals. Getting a gold medal in one level will take practice and a lot of concentration.

    How to play Vex 5?On computer, move your stickman with the arrows on your keyboard, jump with the up arrow and slide with the down arrow when you are in movement. Your hero will be able to lean briefly on the walls to jump, slide by hanging on to ropes, push objects or break certain fragile surfaces.

    What are the game modes of Vex 5?- Normal mode: This mode offers 9 acts and a special level, it will allow you to gradually understand all the mechanics of the game and to train to control your stickman.
    - Hardcore mode: Hardcore levels are unlocked by obtaining gold medals for each act. This mode is extremely demanding and will require flawless precision and agility. Each hardcore level is an improved and much more difficult version of an act.

    Who is the author of Vex 5?The game was created by Amazing Adam, the same developer as Vex 3 and Vex 4.
