    Aground

    Author : Fancy Fish Games  -   336 729 plays

    You are one of the only survivors of humanity, stranded on an island with a few other humans, will you be able to return to a normal life and survive in a hostile universe ? Aground is a 2D survival game with minimalist graphics. Inspired by Utopian Mining, the adventure is based around the progression and evolution of your character. The island is huge, explore every corner and venture into basements in search of precious minerals. Discover a rich scenario that will guide you throughout your journey.
