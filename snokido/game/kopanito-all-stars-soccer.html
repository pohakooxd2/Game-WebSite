    Kopanito All-Stars Soccer

    Author : MerixGames   -   200 299 plays

    To wait for the euro soccer 2016, what better to play one of the best browser soccer game? Kopanito All-Stars Soccer invites you to participate in a tournament with the team of your choice among the 8 available. Once on the ground let speak your collective and technical playing with your teammates by making direct, strained or lobed passes. Once in scoring position, make a shot and try to beat the keeper, the time will slowing down and will allow you to adjust the direction of your shot and make impressive strikes. Super moves will also be available after filling the gauge of players and the defense will not be left with the ability to make spectacular slipped tackles to recover the ball.
