    Temple Run 2

    Author : Imangi Studios  -   60 935 plays

    Run at full speed and explore the ruins of a temple full of riches but also traps in the game Temple Run 2. Your character is being chased by a strange ferocious creature and he must absolutely not stop his course or be devoured. ! Flee through temple ruins, hold on to ropes, jump over sharp pikes and deadly flames, slide under rocks, and take canals filled with whitewater you can hurtle down at full speed. Temple Run 2 is an endless runner game in which you will have to try to travel the longest distance possible with each attempt. Also try to collect a maximum of gold coins, they will be used to improve certain skills. The game also offers daily challenges that will give you gold coins bonuses.
