    Penguin Diner

    Author : 2Dplay | BigWig   -   39 148 plays

    The penguins have decided to open their own restaurants! In Penguin Diner, the time for fishing is over, and it’s now time to meet and greet the hordes of customers flocking to your establishments. Place the customers at a table then wait for them to wave you to take their orders, slide on your ice skates to the bar to pick up the various dishes and collect the money once the customers have left. Penguin Diner will ask you to use your sense of management and timing to serve the penguins on time and earn the necessary turnover. Improve your character and put your skills to use in bigger and bigger restaurants. The game is now playable on all devices, computer, tablet and mobile.
