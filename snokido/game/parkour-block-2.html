    Parkour Block 2

    Author : Poly Games   -   111 215 plays

    Parkour Block 2 is a platform and skill game inspired by parkour maps in Minecraft. The goal will be to reach the portal located at the end of each level by jumping from block to block and without falling into the lava below you. Control your movements with precision so as not to jump beside to the platforms and study the best way to reach the portal as quickly as possible. The time you take to complete a level will determine the number of stars obtained. Parkour Block 2 will offer you many challenges through its 42 levels which will be more and more difficult.
