    Rotate

    Author : Light Wolf Studios  -   38 984 plays

    In the platform game Rotate you are a subject of study that will test a series of deadly pieces filled with traps and other dangers. Your goal will be to reach the exit of each level without dying and for that it will be necessary for you to use the capacity to rotate the levels. Use gravity to cross passages with no exits and avoid the traps placed in your path by skirting them. Rotate offers 16 levels, an editor, achievements to unlock and a Speedrun mode for the most competitive players!
