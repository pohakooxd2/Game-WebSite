    Flash Bash

    Author : Godlimations  -   171 847 plays

    Flash Bash is an old-school fighting game that will really knock your socks off! Turn up the volume, invite your friends over and limber up those fingers to get stuck into battle. The animations may seem a little stilted, but once you get to grips with your character and learn a few combos (there are more than 100), the game comes into its own. Deal blow after blow, and fight in increasingly technical, and even tactical, battles. Attack, defend and block perfectly, as the slightest error could prove fatal. Play away to your heart’s content!
