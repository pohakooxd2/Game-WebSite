    Donut vs Donut

    Author : Kyynele  -   28 102 plays

    Donut vs Donut is a local multiplayer arena game where you can participate in sweet cookie fights up to 4 players simultaneously on the same device. The goal will be to be the last surviving donut in the arena, trying to push others out of the bounds of the platform. The donuts automatically turn on themselves and you will have to hit your opponents with the right timing to push them back in the desired direction. On the edges of the platform there will sometimes be different elements such as bumpers or sharp spikes which will spice up the games. The winner will be the first player to get three stars.
