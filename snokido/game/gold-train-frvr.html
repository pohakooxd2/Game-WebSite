    Gold Train FRVR

    Author : FRVR   -   17 147 plays

    Gold Train FRVR is a relaxing puzzle game in which you will have to connect stations by building rail tracks so that your train can reach its destination safely. Select and arrange the rail tiles to build the railroad according to the terrain you will have to cross. Straight lines, turns, crossings, bridges, various elements will be at your disposal. Along the way will be scattered gold coins, try to collect them all by passing to the right places. To succeed at 100% each level, you will have to build your railroad tracks on all the tiles at your disposal, do it before the end of the countdown to double your gold bonus! The gold coins acquired can be spent in the store to buy new locomotives, wagons or other strongbox.
