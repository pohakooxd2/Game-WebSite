    Sniper Shot: Bullet Time

    Author : GoGoMan   -   85 829 plays

    You are a sniper and in Sniper Shot: Bullet Time you will have to complete a series of missions in which you will have to eliminate all the enemies on the map. Locate your targets, aim with precision and trigger the shot. The bullet time technique will allow you to see in slow motion the path of each bullet and their spectacular impact. Move around the map and avoid enemy fire, you can use platforms to jump very high and try to shoot down your targets while you are in the air.
