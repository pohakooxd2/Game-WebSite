    Dream Restaurant

    Author : Hihoy   -   12 437 plays

    Have you ever dreamed of having your own restaurant? In the game Dream Restaurant you can build and develop your culinary business as you want. The cook is ready to work and it's up to you to serve good meals to the first customers who come to your establishment. Gather all the orders and serve the right dishes at each table. With the money earned you can develop your business by buying new tables, hiring other servers and expanding your restaurant. Thereafter, the dishes you serve will also be more varied. Over time and your experience in catering, you can build a chain of restaurants in your name, manage all your establishments and become the best restaurateur in the country!
