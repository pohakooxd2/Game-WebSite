    Phase 10 Online

    Author : Mattel  -   27 708 plays

    Phase 10 Online is a cards game in which you will have to obtain specific combinations of cards to reach your objective before your opponent. The goal will be to deposit on the board the cards from your hand by making suits or by obtaining cards of the same color, by 3, 4 and more. At each turn you must draw a card in the center of the board as well as deposit one, the goal being to no longer have a card in hand while achieve the requested objectives. Phase 10 Online offers thirty solo levels with increasing difficulty but above all an exciting multiplayer mode (accessible by having completed level 8). You will be able to compete against other players online, earn points and consult the general world ranking.
