    Mr. Superfire

    Author : Inlogic Software   -   21 840 plays

    Help special agent Mr. Superfire defeat the evil forces in this fast-paced shooter that will require good reflexes. Your hero will be able to jump or double jump to dodge enemy projectiles and cross obstacles. But its armament will not be outdone and many pistols or rifles can be used to eliminate your opponents. Mr. Superfire is not a special agent for nothing, he will be able to acquire many special abilities that will help him carry out his missions. The game offers 4 large levels with a different theme and no less than 50 missions during which you can collect keys, gold and equipments. Gold and gear pieces will allow you to upgrade your stuff or buy an ability chest. Keys can be used to unlock war chests which will contain valuable rewards!
