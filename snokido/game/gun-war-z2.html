    Gun War Z2

    Author : IT-Hype  -   9 111 plays

    Eliminate hordes of zombies by firering aboard your helicopter and save the inhabitants in danger to bring them to safety. Gun War Z2 is an easy-to-learn shooter, ideal to relax by annihilating hundreds of zombies with many different weapons such as machine guns or lasers. The game offers 30 missions divided into three leagues, you will have to intervene in many different places because the threat of the undead is spreading everywhere! Many upgrades can be unlocked for your equipment as well as 7 uniquely styled helicopters. Daily challenges, missions and mini-games will allow you to get more rewards. It's time to finally eradicate the scourge of zombies!
