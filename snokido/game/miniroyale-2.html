    Mini Royale: Nations

    Author : Faraway Studios  -   395 540 plays

    Fight to be the last player alive in the shooting game Mini Royale: Nations. Control your parachute to land in the desired area and try to quickly find weapons and other objects before engaging the combat. Track down your opponents on the map and try to be the last survivor to win the game. You can launch grenades, shoot drones to pick up loots, use heals and other bonuses that will help you to take the advantage on other players.

    Game ModesThere are 3 game modes currently live on Mini Royale Nations: Team Deathmatch, Capture the Flag, and Free For All. Join matches of 2-3 minutes with 9 other players to score XP and level up, complete Quests, and earn Battle Pass Points to unlock Hero and Weapon skins. Players can play on public servers or in private rooms. Want to play with a friend? Create a Party to game with up to 2 other players on the same team.

    QuestsQuests are mini tasks that give players prizes upon completion. In Mini Royale, there are Daily Quests, Seasonal Quests, Special Quests, and Brutal Quests. Completing these Quests unlocks Loot skins.

    Clan WarsMini Royale Nations features a new Battle Pass each season. Players can earn Battle Pass points by playing in matches, completing Quests, and winning Clan Wars. Earning Battle Pass points unlocks Battle Pass tiers and prizes like Orbs, $BUTTER, and skins.

    FusionIn the Lab, skins can be fused together to unlock high rarity Heroes and Weapons. Both Premium and Loot skins can be unlocked through Fusion. Fusion recipes are released in waves, and include items of all rarity tiers: Uncommon, Rare, Epic, and Legendary.

    Battle PassMini Royale Nations features a new Battle Pass each season. Players can earn Battle Pass points by playing in matches, completing Quests, and winning Clan Wars. Earning Battle Pass points unlocks Battle Pass tiers and prizes like Orbs, $BUTTER, and skins.
