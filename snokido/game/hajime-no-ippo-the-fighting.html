    Hajime no Ippo - The Fighting

    Author : Treasure  -   118 464 plays

    Follow the first steps of Ippo, a talented boxer who wants to become professional and who will have to fight against many redoubtable opponents to prove his worth. Hajime no Ippo: The Fighting is a boxing game, a punch-out like released in 2002 on Gameboy Advance (GBA) and retracing much of the anime's story of the same name. In the story mode you can relive Ippo's career and face the most iconic boxers of the series like Miyata, Mashiba or Sendo, each with their special techniques and their own fighting styles. As to his victories, Ippo will become stronger and will learn devastating techniques like the mythical Dempsey Roll. The second game mode is the tournament mode, where you can choose the level of your opponents, the number of rounds and the order of your fights. A sparring mode will allow you to train and improve your fighting techniques.
