    How Dare You

    Author : The Shpufa   -   54 752 plays

    No-one should dare disturb a monk while he’s meditating, but an inconsiderate alien actually went and yanked a hair out of the old man’s beard! So, the monk decided to unleash his fury in How Dare You, a distance running game. Run like the wind, jump on your enemies and use your 4 special powers to unbridle your anger. Collect as many coins with each attempt and stock up on upgrades for your monk at the shop.
