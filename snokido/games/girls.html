    Games for girls, but not only!

    Girls' games include creative titles that give pride of place to imagination, but also more realistic games that will allow you to discover certain disciplines while having fun. The girls game's category is also a common space where girls, casual or passionate gamers, can meet themselves to discuss about their favorite games and make friends online. Girls' games will offer, for example, managing restaurants or shops, creating your own clothes or even becoming a veterinarian or doctor for a few hours. Games like Royal Story will allow you to develop a farm and take care of countless animals while in My Dolphin Show 7 you can discover the fabulous dolphins.

    What are the different types of girls games?

    • Pet games will be ideal for learning to take care of pets, dogs or cats, farm animals but they will also allow you to discover many wild species as in the game Tiger Simulator 3D.
    • Fashion games will develop your creative mind and imagination to create trendy clothing lines like in the game Shopaholic Hollywood.
    • Beauty games will allow you to invent haircuts or original makeup looks and to test your creations on models. With the game Modern Frozen Sisters it will be possible to create looks for his favorite heroines!
    • Cooking games will be inspiring to learn how to prepare the best culinary dishes in the world. Discover the innumerable flavors and richness of the cooking thanks in particular to games like Sara's Christmas Meal.
    • Love games will give pride of place to feelings and affection, whether to find a lover or to take care of adorable babies like in the game Baby Hazel: Summer Fun.
