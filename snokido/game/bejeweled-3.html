    Bejeweled 3

    Author : PopCap   -   297 864 plays

    Bejeweled 3 has become the most popular puzzle game in the world and serves as a yardstick for all 3-in-a-row games. The rules are classic and the aim is to line up at least 3 of the same coloured jewels to destroy them. Combine 4 precious stones or more to create explosive or catalyser jewels, which destroy all of the jewels in one colour or take out an entire line. Take a good look at the board to set off chain reactions and impressive combos. This third episode of Bejeweled is the perfect game to train your brain and relax at the same time.
