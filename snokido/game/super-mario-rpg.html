    In his last appearance on Super NES, Mario will try a new register far from the classic platform game that has made it successful before. Indeed, we find here the most famous plumbers in a role play game created by Squaresoft Studio (Final Fantasy). All the Mario universe is found condensed in a huge adventure divided into 6 different zones in which the player will be totally free to explore. Mario will always be in search of Princess Peach, which was captured by Smithy, an evil robot that also flew the 7 stars of the Star Road. He will have to fight formidable opponents and explore a gigantic world full of dangers and surprises.
