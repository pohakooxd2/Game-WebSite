    Prison Escape Puzzle is an escape game collection of Prison Escape, Escape Puzzle: Adventures et Escape Puzzle: New Dawn. Look for clues and objects from each scene and interact with different elements to find ways to escape. Take a good look at your environment and be imaginative in finding the right object combinations that can unlock passages, open doors or operate different things.
