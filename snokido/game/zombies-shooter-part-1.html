    Zombies Shooter Part 1

    Author : GoGoMan   -   26 369 plays

    Zombies Shooter Part 1 is a shooting and survival game in which you will have to defeat rampaging hordes of zombies. To survive against the undead there are those who have a gun and those who don't! You, you are armed to the teeth, ready to wreak havoc by eliminating all enemies without mercy. Use a dozen different weapons during the fights, aim for their heads to kill the zombies in one shot. Some will be small and fast, others will be gigantic and powerful, you can use the mini map to spot where the threat is. Zombies Shooter Part 1 offers dozens of levels, will you manage to survive the countless waves of zombies that will sweep over you?
