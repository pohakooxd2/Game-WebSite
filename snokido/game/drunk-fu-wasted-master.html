    Drunk Fu : Wasted Master

    Author : Rusto Games  -   125 056 plays

    What’s the best thing to do after a rather boozy night partying in the local bar? Order pizza and head home? No! Now’s the perfect time for an old-school pub brawl with the other boozers! Hone your drunk fighting skills in the fighter game, Drunk Fu Wasted Master. Try not to stagger around too much and hit as many regulars as possible in single-player mode. To vary the pleasures, 8 different environments will be available (street of the city, prison, military camp, space capsule and more). Or for even more fun, pick a buddy and play on the same computer, either on the same side or against each other in duel mode.
