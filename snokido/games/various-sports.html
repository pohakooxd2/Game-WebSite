    What are various sports games?

    The various sports games category includes various titles that will allow you to discover eclectic sports disciplines. Some games like Ski Safari will offer you to race down snow-covered slopes at top speed on your skis, avoiding the many obstacles that line the routes. In other games like Skate Hooligans you will have to ride on your skateboard on city's roads crowded with cars. The free various sports games offered on Snokido will also allow you to discover sports such as archery with the game Archery World Tour, rugby, cricket, volleyball and even table tennis with the game Table Tennis Pro.

    What are the different types of various sports games?

    • Tennis games will offer you to play exciting and intense matches on virtual courts as for example in the game Tennis Legends 2016. Here you can play with the biggest stars of this sport and participate in tournaments or challenge your friends in friendly matches by playing together on the same computer.
    • Hockey games will offer you to handle the hockey stick and the puck on ice rinks around the world but also play Air-Hockey games like in Gumball: Disc Duel where the stars of the series will compete in thrilling matches.
    • Boxing games will ask you to face your opponents in rings in 1vs1 matchs. In Boxing Hero: Punch Champions, you will have to try to be agile, fast and tenacious to defeat the best boxers in the world and become your turn. the champion.
