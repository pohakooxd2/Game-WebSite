    Highway Road Racing

    Author : Hit Games  -   203 823 plays

    The big straight roads will be your playground in Highway Road Racing, a driving game in which you will have to try to cover the longest possible distance without crashing. Drive fast at the wheel of your sports vehicle while avoiding other cars. The traffic will sometimes be dense and often vehicles will change way at the last second. Touch the other cars to earn bonus points but watch out for collisions that could destroy your racing car! Highway Road Racing offers three game mode, One Way, Two Way and Time Attack in three different environments, Grand Canyon, Santa Monica and Bryce Canyon. You will be able to unlock no less than 12 vehicles in the store and buy various upgrades for each of them.
