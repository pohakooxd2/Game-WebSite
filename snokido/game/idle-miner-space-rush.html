    Idle Miner Space Rush

    Author : Ubisoft Nano   -   28 618 plays

    Mine crystals on the moon and compete against other players online in Idle Miner Space Rush. Your business has taken a giant step on the moon, you are on this new star to reap many riches. Participate in games lasting 2:30 minutes and try to amass as much wealth as possible during this time. Dig to collect diamonds and return to your base to deposit your loot. You will be able to improve your bag size and your mining skills. The deeper you dig, the more valuable the ores found. Look for intermediate bases to avoid going back to the surface and pay attention to the depths of the mine which will be very dangerous! At the end of the allotted time it will be imperative to return in safety in a base to validate your score.
    Idle Miner Space Rush offers a ranking of the best players online, worldwide and by region. There is also a store that will allow you to buy outfits for your character with the gold earned during your games. The different outfits will increase your hero's basic characteristics, which will allow him to be more effective at mining.
