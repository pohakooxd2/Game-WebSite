    Builder Idle Arcade

    Author : Lipsar Studio   -   34 193 plays

    Build houses and develop your construction site in Builder Idle Arcade, a game mixing idle and simulation. Exploit resources such as wood and stone, craft planks, bricks and use the materials to advance the construction of the house. You will be able to increase your character's abilities, improve the various machines, but also occasionally hire employees who will help you collect resources. Resources can be sold for money or used to build your houses. The first projects entrusted to you will be easy and the houses to build will not require too many resources. But quickly you will have to manage more ambitious constructions which will require good organization and perfect logistics.
